    Kitzbühel - the legend: where skiing was ‘invented’, a venue for regular ski races since 1895 and home to the Streif, for decades the arena for the most famous international race in the Winter World Cup. But it is not just Kitzbühel's fantastic selection of slopes that make it such an read moreKitzbühel - the legend: where skiing was ‘invented’, a venue for regular ski races since 1895 and home to the Streif, for decades the arena for the most famous international race in the Winter World Cup. But it is not just Kitzbühel's fantastic selection of slopes that make it such an irresistible destination for many visitors. The resort's unique blend of traditional and contemporary attracts skiers and celebrities from all over the world. An above-average number of luxury hotels, the highest concentration of award-winning restaurants in Tyrol and an amazing evening scene including numerous events make the winter sports resort and town of Kitzbühel Austria's meeting place for snow society throughout the entire winter.
